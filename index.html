Goals

A not-so-long letter for the Jeff in five years

In the coming five years, I envision myself working in the IT industry, specializing predominantly in front-end development(not evident in this exercise. i apologize). I believe my strengths lie in conceptualizing designs and translating them into various platforms such as systems, websites, and applications. My immediate plan is to use my academic background in the industry to secure a position post-graduation. And as I accumulate experience and financial stability within the field, I aspire to pursue my creative passion specifically Visual Development. My goal is to become a Visual Developer, a creator who breathes life into characters with distinct personalities, stories, and unique appearances. This imaginative process, which I often engage in when my focus drifts away during lessons, involves building homes and environments that align with their aesthetic. Despite my current limitation of not being able to draw them, I foresee a future where these characters and stories manifest in games or films, freeing them from my mind. This is my goal in terms of my career.

On a personal note, I am committed to prioritizing my health, acknowledging a decline in both mental and physical well-being over the past few years. I aim to redefine what it means to be a healthy person (wow), paying careful attention to various aspects of my well-being. In alignment with my creative pursuits, my goal is also to join art conventions. Not only as an admirer but as a contributor, sharing and selling my artwork within the community. Furthermore, I am keen on joining creative movements that allow me to utilize my artistic skills for meaningful and impactful projects.

In terms of relationships, I aspire to show greater affection towards my loved ones, particularly my parents, and improve my communication with them. I simply want my loved ones to feel the love they give me by showing them, and I've been longing for a deeper and more open line of communication with them for a while now. This extends to my friends, where I aim to reciprocate the positive energy they bring into our interactions.

While acknowledging that change may be challenging, particularly for someone with earth signs dominating their astrological chart, I am committed to personal growth. No astrological alignment or mercury retrograde can ruin my improvements.
